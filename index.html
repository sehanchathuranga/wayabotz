competition details

WAYABOTZ is a competition based on open-source platforms organized by the Electronics Society (ESOC) and IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers) Student Branch of the Wayamba University of Sri Lanka. The main purpose of the competition is to bring new technology closer to school children and assist undergraduates to put their knowledge into practice. We hope that this will enable the new generation to enter the new world easily and effectively. The competition will be open to any student but especially targeting university students. The participants will be given a task. They will have to complete the given task using the any open-source within the given time period. Their final presentations will be evaluated based on standard criteria. The finalists will be awarded cash prizes. The final round of the competition will be conducted physically at the university premises
